ADMISSIONS REQUIREMENT

Admission Guidelines

Undergraduate Entry Requirements
  • Minimum of 5 High School Credits (including English and Mathematics)
  • Official High School Transcripts
  • Satisfactory score in Venite Matriculation Exam or global equivalent
  • Proof of English Language Proficiency (IELTS 6.5+ or TOEFL 85+ if applicable)
Postgraduate Entry Requirements
  • Bachelor's Degree from an accredited institution (Minimum GPA 3.0/4.0 or equivalent)
  • Two Academic Letters of Recommendation
  • Statement of Research Purpose / Personal Essay
  • Standardized GRE / GMAT test scores (where requested by program faculty)

SUPPORT ENGINES

Scholarships & Grants

At Venite University, we firmly believe financial barriers should never obstruct academic brilliance. We offer numerous automatic and application-based support packages to foster high-integrity achievements:

Award Coverage: 100% Tuition Fees. Automatically granted to incoming undergraduate students who maintain an outstanding high school GPA (equivalent to A / 3.9+). Renewable annually upon maintaining a 3.7+ CGPA.

Award Coverage: $3,000 - $5,000. Aimed at international candidates from underrepresented territories who have shown remarkable social impact, community advocacy, or athletic dedication. Requires a dedicated essay.

Award Coverage: 50% Tuition Fees & Lab Stipends. Dedicated to female candidates applying for Engineering or Computing programs who display superior potential in coding, electrical robotics, or physical systems.
